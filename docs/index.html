About Evergreen Agro Tec (Private) Limited

Evergreen Agro Tec is a leading climate-smart agribusiness in Sindh, Pakistan, managing 1200 acres of sustainable farmland across our Arab Farm, Sipra Farm, and Shabbir Farm. We specialize in the integrated cultivation of sugarcane, grains, and organic vegetables, powered entirely by renewable solar and biogas energy. By merging high-tech irrigation with circular waste-to-vermicompost systems and on-farm processing, we transform raw harvests into premium value-added products like jaggery and dehydrated produce. Our mission is to redefine Pakistani agriculture through resource efficiency, land restoration, and a steadfast commitment to a carbon-neutral food future.

Projects

Farm Restoration & Climate-Smart Agriculture

This project restored approximately 1,200 acres of degraded farmland using regenerative and climate-smart agriculture. Drip and flood irrigation systems powered by solar energy, biogas, and diesel backup were implemented to address water stress and ensure reliability. High-value crops were cultivated to improve soil health, water efficiency, and farm income through practical, sustainable solutions.

Renewable Energy & Biogas Integration

This project integrated renewable energy systems to support sustainable farm operations by producing biogas from Napier grass and cow dung for water lifting and on-farm energy needs. Solar-powered pumps were used during daytime with diesel backup to ensure uninterrupted irrigation. Biogas slurry was converted into vermicompost, improving soil fertility while reducing dependence on chemical fertilizers and lowering the farm’s carbon footprint.

Processing & Value Addition

This initiative established a processing and value-addition facility at Shabbir Farm to convert raw agricultural produce into market-ready products. Sugarcane was processed into jaggery, wheat into flour, rice into polished rice, and onion, chili, and tomato into dried products. The facility supports both B2C and B2B markets, reduces post-harvest losses, and creates income opportunities for women and youth through local processing and packaging.
